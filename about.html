About Me

Clark Fennell

Web & Software Developer

Born in the small town of Harold Wood in Essex, I grew up with my parents and younger brother. I discovered a love for computer games and all things technical, which sparked a thirst for computer sciences at Brentwood Grammar School. This is something I pursued until the age of 18, when I started moving away from Essex and into London, to search for a career which would develop these talents.

Always expanding on my knowledge, I found my creativity and logical approach to development a good fit for the freelance world. I set out to put these skills to use and moved up to Leeds, where I managed to find a great many opportunities.

I have been developing web and software-based services throughout my career, but have expanded my knowledge base vastly in more recent years.

Constantly learning new languages and keeping up with developments, I feel I'm ready for any challenge.

Currently, my position is working in Sheffield for a digital agency called Hydra Creative

I am now ready for my next move, hoping to find a position that will enable me to continue growing my knowledge, and provide me with a fresh challenge. If you are interested in my work, pleasecontact me here.
